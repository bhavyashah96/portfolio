Fill and Seal Machine (Volumetric Filling)

Description:

Is Ideal for filling wide range of dry free flowing products The Machine can handle Detergent Powder, Sugar, whole Spices, Dry Syrup, Coffee Powder and Seeds, Pan Masala, Chemical Powders, almost many others. Operation: The machines operate on the principle of volumetric Portioning, which is achieved by telescopic cups to absorb the batch variation of bulk density. The machine Discharge the set quantity of product into the pouch / container held manually under the discharge hopper.

Specification:

Capacity: 15cc to 1500 cc volume per discharge
Operating speed: Upto 20 fills per minute depending on packsize
Filling Accuracy: Upto +/- 1.5% average, depending upon Consistency of bulk density and packsize
Power: 0.5 HP., 3 phase, 440 v, 50Hz.
Overall Dimensions: 800mmm x 800mm x 1500mm (L x W x H )
Net Weight: 150 kg. Approximately
